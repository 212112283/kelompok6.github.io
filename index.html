About me

I was born on June 28, 1971 in South Africa. My father, Errol Musk, is an electromechanical engineer, while his mother, Maye Musk, is a Canadian model and nutritionist.I am known as an entrepreneur who is listed as one of the richest people in the world. I am also considered an innovator who creates contemporary inventions.

I became interested in studying computer engineering. At 12 years old, I learned programming and managed to build and sell it for $500. I graduated from Pretoria Boys High School. In 1992, after studying for two years at Queen's University, Kingston, Ontario, then transferred to the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and earned a bachelor's degree in economics and a second bachelor's degree in physics. to California to pursue a Ph.D. in applied physics at Stanford but left after two days pursuing his passion for entrepreneurship in the Internet, renewable energy, and aerospace sectors.
